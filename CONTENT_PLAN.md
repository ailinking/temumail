# Content Strategy & Editorial Calendar for TemuMail

## 1. Content Mission
To become the most trusted and practical resource for online privacy, digital hygiene, and temporary communication tools. Content will be authoritative, actionable, and human-centric, avoiding generic "AI fluff."

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Privacy Conscious Users**: People worried about data tracking and breaches.
*   **Developers/QA**: Tech professionals needing test emails for authentication flows.
*   **Bargain Hunters**: Users signing up for discounts/trials who want to avoid subsequent spam.
*   **General Internet Users**: People tired of a cluttered inbox.

## 4. Content Guidelines (Anti-AI Policy)
*   **Tone**: Conversational, direct, and empathetic. Use "we" and "you."
*   **Structure**: Short paragraphs, clear H2/H3 headers, bullet points for readability.
*   **Fact-Checking**: Verify technical claims (e.g., how email protocols work).
*   **Value Add**: Every post must offer a concrete solution or insight, not just definitions.
*   **Linking**: Interlink between posts to create a "knowledge web."
